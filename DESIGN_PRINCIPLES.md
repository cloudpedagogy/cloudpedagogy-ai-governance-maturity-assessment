# Design Principles: AI Governance Maturity Assessment

## Tone & Voice
- **Institutional**: Serious but accessible.
- **Reflective**: Encourages deep thought rather than quick answers.
- **Non-Judgemental**: Focuses on identification and improvement, not ranking or competition.
- **Structured**: Provides a clear, logical path through the assessment.

## UI/UX Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity**: Simple, clean interfaces.
- **Reflection over Automation**: Use prompts to guide thinking.
- **Premium Aesthetic**: Clean typography, subtle animations, and a professional color palette.
- **Offline First**: Works without an internet connection.

## Governance Rigour
- All assessments are based on well-defined governance dimensions.
- Outcomes are interpretive and focus on structural readiness.
- Exports are structured for institutional use.
