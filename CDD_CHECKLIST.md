# CDD Checklist: AI Governance Maturity Assessment

## Capability Intent
- [ ] Explicitly defines the capability being assessed (AI Governance Maturity).
- [ ] Focuses on readiness and structural integrity rather than just list of tools.

## Reflective Scaffolding
- [ ] Embedded reflective prompts in each dimension.
- [ ] Encourages qualitative notes alongside quantitative scoring.

## Evidence of Governance
- [ ] Output provides a clear, structured maturity profile.
- [ ] Gaps and weaknesses are explicitly highlighted.
- [ ] Improvement directions are suggested based on scores.

## Institutional Context
- [ ] Tone is formal and institutional.
- [ ] Export formats support professional reporting (MD, PDF).
